Frequently Asked Questions

How much bone broth should I give my dog?

Small dogs (under 20 lbs): 1-2 ounces daily. Medium dogs (20-50 lbs): 2-4 ounces daily. Large dogs (50-80 lbs): 4-8 ounces daily. Extra-large dogs (80+ lbs): 8-12 ounces daily. Start with smaller amounts and increase gradually.

Should I make bone broth or buy it?

Both work. Homemade gives you complete control and is more cost-effective. Store-bought is convenient but often lower quality. Look for products that gel when refrigerated and list bones as the first ingredient.

Is bone broth safe for all dogs?

Generally yes, but use caution with kidney disease (high phosphorus). Start slowly and watch for digestive upset. It should complement a balanced diet, not replace it.
