About Watts

Real nutrition for a longer, better life.

We believe the habits that extend human healthspan apply to dogs too: small, consistent choices, compounded over time. Watts was created to give dogs a daily ritual rooted in whole-food nutrition—not synthetics, fillers, or shortcuts.

The Problem We're Solving

Most dog supplements aren't actually built for a dog's body.

They're made from:

  • Synthetic vitamins the body barely absorbs
  • 60–80% fillers and additives
  • Artificial flavors to make them palatable
  • Nutrient profiles that omit essential building blocks dogs actually need

Dog parents think they're doing the right thing. But the category is built for shelf stability, margin, and taste—not long-term vitality.

Our Core Insight

Real food delivers real results.

Dogs evolved to absorb nutrients from meat and organs—not lab-made powders. Natural, animal-based nutrition is simply more usable by their bodies, leading to 3–10x greater bioavailability and more meaningful health benefits over time.

Our Approach

Watts is a premium dog treat made from grass-fed beef and organs, air-dried to preserve maximum nutrition. Every bite delivers:

  • Natural, bioactive nutrients your dog's body can actually use
  • Heme iron, taurine, Vitamin A, zinc, K2, and carnitine
  • Zero fillers, synthetics, or artificial ingredients
  • The kind of nutrition that compounds over time into better energy, immunity, and vitality

Our Standard

Transparency, simplicity, integrity—all the way down to the label.

We use only real, whole-food ingredients. No synthetic vitamins. No seed oils. No fillers, binders, or artificial flavors. No by-products, wheat, corn, or soy.

Just real nutrition your dog can feel.
