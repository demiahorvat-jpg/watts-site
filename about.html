Why "Watts"?

A watt is a measure of power—the rate at which energy is transferred and put to work.

We chose the name because we're not interested in nutrition that just looks good on a label. We care about nutrition that actually powers your dog's body: absorbed, utilized, and converted into real energy, resilience, and vitality.

Most supplements focus on ingredient lists and milligram counts. We focus on what happens after your dog eats—whether those nutrients make it into the bloodstream, reach the cells that need them, and do the work they're supposed to do.

That's the difference between nutrition on paper and nutrition that generates real power.

The Problem We're Solving

Commercial dog food is formulated to meet AAFCO nutritional minimums—standards designed to prevent deficiency, not to optimize health, support changing needs, or account for how nutrients are actually absorbed and used by the body.

AAFCO doesn't evaluate ingredient quality, sourcing, processing methods, or biological context. Nutritional adequacy is determined by formulation and lab analysis alone—not by what a dog's body can actually do with those nutrients.

Even high-quality food is built to be shelf-stable and consistent at scale. That means it's processed, uniform, and designed to deliver the same nutrition every day regardless of life stage, activity level, stress, growth, or aging.

Supplements exist to add back what modern feeding removes: flexibility, freshness, and whole-food nutrition that adapts to your dog's real, changing needs—without requiring you to overhaul their entire diet.

Our Approach

Watts is built on what we call Native Biome principles—a framework that puts your dog's biology first.

Biology-led formulation
We design around how a dog's body actually digests, absorbs, and uses nutrients—not around high-dose ingredients meant to force a response.

Real food foundation
Whole, grass-fed meats and recognizable ingredients do the heavy lifting. No synthetic additives pretending to be food.

Functionals that earn their place
Added ingredients serve a real, evidence-backed purpose—not marketing hype.

Clean by design
No fillers, sweeteners, glycerin, cellulose, or synthetic binders. Just what's needed, nothing extra.

Made to be used by the body
We focus on nutrients your dog can absorb and utilize, not impressive-looking numbers on a label.

What Makes Watts Different

Starts with food, not powders
Most supplements begin with isolated extracts and synthetic vitamins, then add flavors, sweeteners, and binders to make them edible. Watts starts with real meat dogs instinctively want to eat—and builds nutrition into the food itself.

Nutrition delivered through protein
We use a high-protein format instead of sugar-based chews or filler-heavy tablets, aligning with how dogs are biologically designed to eat and digest.

Air-dried, not ultra-processed
Many pet supplements are made with high-heat processing that degrades natural nutrients and requires synthetic vitamins to be added back. Watts is gently air-dried to preserve what's already there.

Designed for daily use, not forced effects
Instead of relying on megadoses meant to create a quick response, Watts is built for consistent, everyday support of long-term health.

Clean by design
No glycerin, cellulose, artificial flavors, dyes, or sweeteners used to mask bad ingredients or hold powders together.

Who Watts Is For

Watts is for dog parents who want to feed better without changing how they feed.

Most dogs eat modern, shelf-stable food. Watts is designed to layer real, functional nutrition on top of whatever food you already trust—making it easy to support your dog's health without overhauling your routine.

Watts is especially relevant for dogs whose needs shift over time: during growth, aging, pregnancy or nursing, high activity, stress, recovery, or dietary transitions.
