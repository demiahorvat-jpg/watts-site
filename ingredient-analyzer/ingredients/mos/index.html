Scientific Evidence

Function and Purpose

Primary Function: Prebiotic yeast cell wall component supporting gut health and immune function

Nutritional Profile and Composition

Mannan-oligosaccharides (MOS) are complex carbohydrates derived from the outer cell wall of yeast (typically Saccharomyces cerevisiae). MOS consist of mannose sugars linked in chains, with a structure that allows them to bind to certain pathogenic bacteria, preventing their attachment to intestinal walls.

Beyond pathogen binding, MOS modulate immune function through interaction with immune cells in gut-associated lymphoid tissue. They also support beneficial bacteria and may improve intestinal barrier function. MOS are not digested by mammalian enzymes but interact with gut microbiota and immune cells.

Efficacy and Research

Research in dogs demonstrates that MOS supplementation can reduce pathogenic bacteria (E. coli, Salmonella), support beneficial microbiota, improve immune markers, and enhance digestive health. Studies show improved stool quality, reduced diarrhea incidence in puppies, and enhanced vaccine response.

Typical inclusion rates range from 0.1-0.5% of diet, with effects developing over 2-4 weeks of consistent use. MOS work synergistically with probiotics and other prebiotics. They're particularly valuable in puppy foods, immune support formulas, and products targeting digestive health. Well-tolerated with minimal side effects.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is mos safe for dogs?

Yes, mos is generally safe for dogs when used appropriately in properly formulated dog food. Quality and appropriateness can vary based on sourcing, processing, and your individual dog's needs. Monitor your dog for any sensitivities when introducing new foods, and consult your veterinarian if you have specific health concerns.

What does mos do in dog products?

Prebiotic fiber derived from yeast cell walls that supports immune function and blocks pathogen binding in the gut. Dog food manufacturers include this ingredient to provide prebiotic for beneficial bacteria and provide blocks pathogen binding to gut wall.

What is MOS and what does it do?

MOS (mannan-oligosaccharides) are prebiotics derived from yeast cell walls that support gut health and immune function. MOS work by binding to harmful bacteria, preventing them from attaching to intestinal walls, and promoting beneficial bacteria growth. Research shows MOS can reduce diarrhea, support immune response, and improve overall gut health in dogs. MOS are commonly used in pet foods as functional ingredients supporting digestive and immune health. They're generally safe and beneficial, particularly for puppies, senior dogs, or dogs with digestive sensitivities.
