What It Is

Synthetic antioxidant preservative chemically similar to BHA.

Why It's Used in Dog Products

Manufacturers include bht in dog food, treats, and supplements for several reasons:

  • Prevents fat oxidation
  • Extends shelf life
  • Cheaper than natural options

Key Considerations

When evaluating bht in dog products, it's important to understand natural versus synthetic options, safety profile, and effectiveness. This ingredient's quality and appropriateness can vary significantly based on sourcing, processing, and the specific formula it's used in.

Quality Note

Synthetic preservative with potential health concerns similar to BHA.

Potential Concerns

While bht can be appropriate in dog nutrition, pet owners should be aware of potential health impacts, regulatory approval, and consumer preferences. Individual dogs may respond differently to the same ingredient based on their health status, age, and sensitivities.

Watts' Take

We avoid BHT. Natural alternatives exist and are preferable.
