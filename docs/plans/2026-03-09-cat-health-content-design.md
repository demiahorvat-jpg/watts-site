# Cat Health Content Strategy Design

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Status:** Approved
**Timeline:** 3-6 months until cat product launch

## Goals

1. **SEO/organic traffic** — Rank for cat health keywords to capture cat owners searching for information
2. **Brand positioning** — Establish Watts as a brand that deeply understands feline health, not a dog brand that added cat products

## Core Principles

### Positioning
Watts is a brand that deeply understands feline biology and what supports cat health over a lifetime. Supplements are one tool in that picture.

### Content Philosophy
- **Cats are the main character** — No dog comparisons, no "unlike dogs, cats..." framing. Content should read as if Watts started as a cat brand.
- **Biology-first, not ingredient-first** — Lead with how cats work, not what's in the supplement
- **Health supplement positioning** — Watts is a health supplement brand, not a nutritional supplement brand. Content covers cat biology and health broadly, not just nutrition.
- **Confident expertise** — Respects that cat owners take their cats' health seriously

### Brand Voice (per docs/brand-voice-guide.md)
- Alive, not polished
- Clever, not cute (no cutesy cat puns)
- Confident, not preachy
- Real, not clinical
- Use "use" not "utilize"
- Use "quality" not "premium"
- No vague hedging when evidence supports direct claims

## Content Architecture

### Hub Page
**"Cat Health"** — Overview that establishes the Watts framework for thinking about feline health, introduces the major systems, links out to pillar guides. Positioning piece, not comprehensive guide.

### Pillar Guides (6 total)
Each health pillar gets its own comprehensive, standalone guide:

| Pillar | Guide Title | Focus |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| Immune System | Guide to Feline Immune Health | How the cat immune system works, what supports it |
| Gut Health | Guide to Cat Gut Health | Feline digestion, microbiome, absorption |
| Kidney & Organ Health | Guide to Cat Kidney & Organ Health | Supporting kidney, liver, heart function |
| Aging & Longevity | Guide to Cat Aging & Longevity | What happens as cats age, supporting healthspan |
| Metabolic Health | Guide to Cat Metabolic Health | Weight, energy, blood sugar regulation |
| Skin, Coat & Inflammation | Guide to Cat Skin & Coat Health | Inflammatory response, allergies, external health signs |

### Blog Post Clusters
Each pillar guide gets 3-5 supporting blog posts targeting specific search queries.

## Phased Rollout

### Phase 1 (Months 1-2): Foundation
- Hub page: "Cat Health" overview
- Pillar guides:
  - Feline Immune Health
  - Cat Gut Health
- 6-8 blog posts (3-4 per pillar)

### Phase 2 (Months 3-4): Expand
- Pillar guides:
  - Cat Kidney & Organ Health
  - Cat Aging & Longevity
- 6-8 blog posts (3-4 per pillar)

### Phase 3 (Month 5+): Deepen
- Remaining pillar guides:
  - Cat Metabolic Health
  - Cat Skin & Coat Health
- Fill in blog post gaps
- Add cat-specific context to ingredient analyzer pages

## SEO Requirements

Every piece of content must include:
- Keyword research before writing (primary + secondary keywords)
- Optimized title tag and meta description
- Proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2s, H3s)
- Internal linking to related cat content
- Schema markup (FAQ, Article, BreadcrumbList as appropriate)
- Target featured snippets where applicable

## Quality Bar

- Scientifically accurate with citations where needed
- Genuinely useful (not keyword-stuffed)
- Better than whatever currently ranks #1 for target keywords
- Passes brand voice guide standards
